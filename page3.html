T-B: Phase AI and Robotics Engineer (2022)

I have experience working in a professional R&D environment during my role at
T-B:Phase – a joint swarm robotics venture between Thales and the University of Bristol. My role leaned towards the engineering end of the team – in which I was responsible for defining the behaviour of AI agents based on that of a machine learning model, and porting said behaviour to a fleet of networked physical robots. This was achieved using a combination of backend languages including C, C++, and Python; facilitated within the ROS (Robot Operating System) framework and using the MMQT message architecture pattern.

My role was as one member of an inter-disciplinary team. As such, I have real world
experience in agile software development using modern practices and tools including JIRA and Git. 
In my last performance review, my T-B:Phase project coordinator described me as
driven, confident, friendly and communicative, as well as a strong problem solver.

A research paper is currently being written, for which I shall be a co-author.
